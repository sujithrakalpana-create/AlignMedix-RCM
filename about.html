About AlignMedix RCM

A provider-first RCM partner built on experience, integrity, and compassion for the healthcare community.

Who We Are

At AlignMedix RCM, we believe that every healthcare provider deserves seamless financial operations and a reliable support system behind the scenes.

Our team brings 10+ years of combined experience across U.S. medical billing, coding, AR follow-up, denials, QA, and AI-enhanced workflows. We focus on helping solo physicians and small practices strengthen collections while reducing administrative stress.

We operate with transparency, ethical practices, and a deep understanding of the pressures clinics face—so you can focus on what matters most: quality patient care.

Mission
To empower healthcare providers with dependable, accurate, and ethical RCM services that improve reimbursements and create a calm, predictable financial workflow.
Vision
To become the most trusted, provider-focused RCM partner for small and mid-sized practices worldwide—known for compassion, precision, and long-term partnership.
Core Values
Integrity, accuracy, transparency, compassion, and continuous improvement in every process and interaction.
How We Work
We integrate with your EMR and current processes, provide clear reporting, and communicate proactively so you’re always informed and in control.
