About AlignMedix RCM

Aligning healthcare revenue with precision, trust, and 10+ years of expert RCM experience.

Who We Are

AlignMedix RCM is a professional revenue cycle management partner dedicated to helping healthcare practices achieve financial stability with clean processes, compliant workflows, and transparent communication.

Our leadership brings 10+ years of experience in U.S. medical billing, AR management, denials, credentialing, and practice support. We understand the operational and financial pressures faced by solo providers and small practices, and we are committed to delivering accurate, reliable, and timely support.

With a small, highly experienced team, we work as an extension of your practice—ensuring consistency, accountability, and a smooth revenue cycle that allows providers to focus fully on patient care.

Mission
To provide healthcare providers with dependable, accurate, and ethical RCM services that improve reimbursements, reduce administrative load, and create a predictable financial workflow.
Vision
To be the most trusted RCM partner for small and mid-sized practices—recognized for precision, transparency, compassionate support, and long-term value creation.
Core Values
Integrity in every interaction, accuracy in every claim, transparency in communication, and a continuous commitment to improvement and client satisfaction.
How We Work
We integrate seamlessly with your existing EMR systems, follow structured workflows, provide frequent updates, and ensure clear reporting so you always stay informed and in control.
